Linn Kazoo just got better

Fast and easy to use, the new Linn Kazoo is available on mobile, tablet or desktop and now includes these incredible features:

  • Full integration of your iTunes library
  • Direct access to your music collection via USB drives
  • Refined multi-room access

Seamless iTunes Integration

Access your full iTunes library from Linn Kazoo and experience your favourite tracks in the highest quality possible; whilst still being able to organise your music in iTunes.

Easy Access to your USB drives

Simply connect any USB drive to your NAS or computer running Linn Kazoo Server to start browsing and searching the music on your drive, whilst keeping your main music collection separate; no configuration required!

New and Improved Multiroom Experience

Multi-room streaming is now easier than ever to use with Linn Kazoo. With integration into the room selection area, it is never been simpler to group rooms and enjoy music throughout your home!
